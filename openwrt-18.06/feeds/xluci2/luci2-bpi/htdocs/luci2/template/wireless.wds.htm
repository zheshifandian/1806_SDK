<%:Wi-Fi Repeater Setting%>

<%:With Wi-Fi Repeater, master and slave routers can be wirelessly bridged to extend the coverage of the wireless network. %>
<%:After the Wi-Fi Repeater function is enabled, the local router functions as the slave router for Wi-Fi Repeater networking.%>

<%:Wi-Fi Repeater%>
<%:1. Click the top button to enter the wireless scanning page, select a wireless and enter the password to connect%>
<%:2. Click connect and wait for the connection to complete.The page automatically refreshes to check if the connection is successful, and if so, the Wi-Fi Repeater Settings are automatically set.%>
<%:3. After the Wi-Fi Repeater function is enabled, you can view the wireless information about the upper-level route and the local router on the page. Click the upper button again to disable the Wi-Fi Repeater function.%>
